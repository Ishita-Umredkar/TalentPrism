# TalentPrism Master Ontology

The candidate discovery and ranking system organizes the 17 raw Job Description (JD) constraints into 8 conceptual categories. These categories span technical expertise, engineering rigor, domain experience, career stability, public validation, and hiring logistics.

---

## 1. Retrieval & Search Systems Expertise
Measures hands-on capabilities in building vector search databases and information retrieval ranking pipelines.
*   **MH1**: Production experience with embedding-based retrieval and vector databases (Pinecone, Weaviate, Milvus, Qdrant, OpenSearch, FAISS).
*   **MH2**: Hands-on experience designing offline/online evaluation frameworks for ranking systems (NDCG, MRR, MAP, A/B testing).

## 2. Production ML Engineering
Evaluates software engineering fundamentals and the capability to scale and deploy end-to-end ML pipelines.
*   **MH3**: Proven track record of shipping end-to-end machine learning systems to production.
*   **MH4**: Strong Python programming skills, code quality, and engineering standards.
*   **Rejection 1**: Gaps in production coding (excluding purely academic or theoretical research backgrounds).

## 3. LLM & Modern AI Expertise
Measures domain knowledge of large language models, neural ranking, and deep learning architectures.
*   **Preferred 1**: Advanced LLM fine-tuning techniques (PEFT, QLoRA) and learning-to-rank setups.
*   **Rejection 2**: Depth of ML foundations (excluding superficial API wrapper/prompt-only usage).

## 4. Product & Domain Experience
Tracks experience inside product-focused engineering organizations rather than outsourcing setups.
*   **Preferred 2**: Matching system experience and HR-tech / recommendation systems domain knowledge.
*   **Rejection 4 (Positive Component)**: Background building and scaling in-house features for product companies.

## 5. Career Quality & Stability
Evaluates candidate career health, tenure consistency, and coding involvement in recent roles.
*   **Negative 1**: Pattern of frequent job hopping or short tenures (average company stay < 1.5 years).
*   **Rejection 3**: Active hands-on coding contribution (excluding purely hands-off architects or managers).
*   **Rejection 4 (Negative Component)**: Non-product services background (excluding exclusively IT consulting backgrounds).

## 6. Technical Breadth & Specialization
Filters for stack alignment and domain-specific roles.
*   **Rejection 5**: Core focus in NLP and Information Retrieval (excluding mismatched specializations like Computer Vision, Speech, or Robotics).

## 7. External Validation
Checks for public verification of candidate capabilities.
*   **Preferred 3**: Documented public contributions to open-source AI/ML projects on GitHub.
*   **Rejection 6**: Work transparency and public verification (excluding closed-source only track records without external validation).

## 8. Hiring Readiness
Evaluates logistical alignment, location match, and notice period constraints.
*   **MH5**: Geographic location compatibility (Noida, Pune, Delhi NCR, Mumbai, Hyderabad).
*   **Preferred 4**: Join timeline and immediate/short notice period status (notice period <= 30 days).
*   **Negative 2**: Recruiter responsiveness, platform activity, and outreach engagement.
